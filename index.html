What is $ANSEM?

$ANSEM, known as "The Black Bull," is a memecoin on the Solana blockchain — a community-driven cryptocurrency with no company behind it, traded entirely on decentralized exchanges. It launched on Pump.fun in June 2026 and runs on Solana, a blockchain known for fast, sub-cent transactions.

Ansem (@blknoiz06) is one of the most-followed crypto traders on X, best known for calling Solana early and for his influence over Solana memecoin culture. The $ANSEM token began as a community-created tribute to him — and he has since embraced it, actively promoting the token and airdropping it to his followers.

How do I buy $ANSEM?

You need a Solana wallet (such as Phantom) with some SOL in it — SOL is Solana's main coin, and you can buy it on any major exchange. Then swap SOL for $ANSEM using the Buy $ANSEM button at the top of this page, and always check that the contract address matches the one shown above before you trade.

Is $ANSEM safe to invest in?

Memecoins are extremely volatile and speculative — prices can (and often do) go to zero. Nothing on this page is financial advice. Verify the contract address, do your own research, and never invest money you can't afford to lose.

What is a contract address?

Every token on Solana has one unique on-chain ID — the long code shown at the top of this page. Scammers launch fake tokens with the same name, so the address is the only reliable way to know you're buying the real Black Bull.
